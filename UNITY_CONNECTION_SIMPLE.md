1. **Delete** the contents of `D:\UnityProjects\potatolegends` (keep the folder)

   - Switch to branch: **"unity-mobile-game"**
3. **Copy ALL files** from the cloned repo into `D:\UnityProjects\potatolegends`

- Your existing work will be preserved and enhanced!

2. Click "Open" 
3. Select: `D:\UnityProjects\potatolegends`
4. Unity 6000.2.2f1 will open the project!

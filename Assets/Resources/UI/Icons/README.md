## 📐 **Recommended Sizes:**
- **Small Icons:** 64x64px or 128x128px
- **Navigation Icons:** 128x128px
- **Battle Icon:** 128x128px or 256x256px (more prominent)

## 🎨 **Format:**
- **PNG with transparency**
- **Square aspect ratio** (1:1)
- **Consistent art style** across all icons
- **High contrast** for mobile visibility

## 🔧 **Import Settings:**
After adding your icons:
1. Select the icon in Unity
2. Inspector → Texture Type → "Sprite (2D and UI)"
3. Click "Apply"

## ✅ **Automatic Integration:**
The ProductionUIManager will automatically detect and use your custom icons when you add them to this folder!

# 💎 CARD RARITY BORDER ASSETS

Upload your custom card rarity borders here!

## 🎨 Rarity Border Assets:

### **Required Rarity Borders:**
- `common-border.png` - Gray/White border for common cards
- `uncommon-border.png` - Green border for uncommon cards
- `rare-border.png` - Blue border for rare cards
- `legendary-border.png` - Purple border for legendary cards
- `exotic-border.png` - Gold/Rainbow border for exotic cards

### **📏 Specifications:**
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Size:** Same as card frame (120x160 pixels)
- **Style:** Border/frame overlay that goes around card edges
- **Transparency:** Inner area should be transparent to show card content

### **🎯 Visual Effect:**
```
Common Card:          Legendary Card:
┌─[common-border]─┐   ┌─[legendary-border]─┐
│ 🔥 Fire Potato  │   │ 👑 Dragon Lord     │
│ Gray border     │   │ Purple/Gold border │
└─────────────────┘   └────────────────────┘
```

### **🌈 Color Recommendations:**
- **Common:** Light gray or white (#CCCCCC)
- **Uncommon:** Forest green (#228B22)
- **Rare:** Royal blue (#4169E1)
- **Legendary:** Deep purple (#8A2BE2)
- **Exotic:** Gold gradient (#FFD700 to #FFA500)

### **🚀 Usage:**
These borders overlay on top of your card frames to indicate rarity at a glance!

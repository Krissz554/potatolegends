# 🌟 ELEMENTAL CARD BACKGROUND ASSETS

Upload your custom elemental card backgrounds here!

## 🔥 Required Elemental Backgrounds:

### **Element Types:**
- `fire-card-bg.png` - Fire element cards (red/orange theme)
- `ice-card-bg.png` - Ice element cards (blue/cyan theme)
- `lightning-card-bg.png` - Lightning element cards (yellow/purple theme)
- `light-card-bg.png` - Light element cards (white/gold theme)
- `void-card-bg.png` - Void element cards (dark/purple theme)
- `exotic-card-bg.png` - Exotic element cards (rainbow/special theme)

### **📏 Recommended Specifications:**
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Size:** 120x160 pixels (card size)
- **Style:** Background texture/pattern for each element
- **Transparency:** Edges can be transparent for custom shapes

### **🎨 Elemental Themes:**

#### 🔥 **Fire Element:**
- Colors: Red, orange, yellow
- Effects: Flames, lava, heat waves
- Mood: Aggressive, powerful

#### ❄️ **Ice Element:**
- Colors: Light blue, cyan, white
- Effects: Ice crystals, frost, snow
- Mood: Cool, defensive

#### ⚡ **Lightning Element:**
- Colors: Yellow, purple, electric blue
- Effects: Lightning bolts, energy, sparks
- Mood: Fast, energetic

#### 🌟 **Light Element:**
- Colors: White, gold, pale yellow
- Effects: Rays, halos, divine light
- Mood: Pure, healing

#### 🌀 **Void Element:**
- Colors: Dark purple, black, deep blue
- Effects: Swirls, darkness, mystical
- Mood: Mysterious, powerful

#### ✨ **Exotic Element:**
- Colors: Rainbow, iridescent, multi-color
- Effects: Prismatic, magical, unique
- Mood: Rare, special

## 🔧 **HOW TO UPLOAD:**

### **Step 1: Copy to Unity Project**
1. **Copy your asset files** to the exact folder paths above
2. **Unity will automatically detect** them

### **Step 2: Set Import Settings**
For each asset:
1. **Select the asset** in Unity Project window
2. **Inspector → Texture Type** → **"Sprite (2D and UI)"**
3. **Click "Apply"**

### **Step 3: Test**
- **Press Play** in Unity
- **Check Unity Console** for messages like:
  ```
  ✅ Found custom gold icon
  ✅ Found custom main menu background
  ✅ Found custom battle icon
  ```

## 📐 **ASSET SPECIFICATIONS:**

### **🎨 Icons (Square):**
- **Size:** 64x64px, 128x128px, or 256x256px
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Style:** Consistent with your game theme
- **Colors:** High contrast for mobile visibility

### **🖼️ Backgrounds (Portrait):**
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (recommended)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Style:** Fantasy/Elemental theme
- **Quality:** High resolution for mobile

### **🔘 Buttons (Rectangular):**
- **Size:** 300x100px (primary), 400x120px (battle)
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Style:** 9-slice ready borders
- **Design:** Fantasy theme with good contrast
